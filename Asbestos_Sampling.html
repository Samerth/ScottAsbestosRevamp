Asbestos Sampling

What is Asbestos Sampling?

Asbestos sampling is the process of collecting samples from various materials suspected to contain asbestos for laboratory analysis. It involves careful collection and handling to ensure accurate results and proper risk assessment.

Our Asbestos Sampling Process

  1. Survey: Initial assessment to identify materials suspected to contain asbestos.
  2. Sample Collection: Careful collection of samples following strict protocols to minimize contamination.
  3. Laboratory Analysis: Samples are sent to accredited laboratories for thorough analysis to determine asbestos content.
  4. Reporting: Detailed reports outlining asbestos presence and concentration levels.
  5. Recommendations: Based on results, recommendations for further action are provided.
