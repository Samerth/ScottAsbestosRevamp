Mould Inspection

Mould inspection is a vital process aimed at identifying the presence of mould growth within indoor environments. Mould, a type of fungus, thrives in damp, moist areas and can proliferate rapidly if left unchecked. Mould inspection involves a thorough assessment of buildings, homes, or indoor spaces to locate areas of mould growth, determine the extent of infestation, and identify underlying moisture issues contributing to mould development.

At Scott Asbestos and Hazardous Removal Ltd., our mould inspection services are conducted by experienced professionals trained to detect and assess mould contamination accurately. Using advanced techniques and equipment, we conduct comprehensive inspections to identify mould hotspots, assess the severity of infestation, and provide recommendations for effective remediation.

Indoor Air Quality Testing

Indoor air quality (IAQ) testing is a comprehensive evaluation of the air within buildings, homes, or indoor spaces to determine the presence of pollutants, allergens, and other contaminants. Poor indoor air quality can arise from various sources, including mould, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), dust, pet dander, and environmental factors like humidity and ventilation. IAQ testing involves assessing the concentration levels of these pollutants to ensure occupants are breathing clean and healthy air.

At Scott Asbestos and Hazardous Removal Ltd., our indoor air quality testing services encompass a range of analyses and assessments tailored to your specific needs. We utilize advanced equipment and methodologies to detect and measure indoor air pollutants accurately, providing actionable insights to improve air quality and create a healthier indoor environment.
