Lead Sampling

What is Lead Sampling?

Lead sampling is the process of collecting samples from various surfaces or materials to determine the presence and concentration of lead contaminants.

Our Lead Sampling Process

  1. Surface Selection: Identifying areas and materials suspected of containing lead.
  2. Sampling Method: Employing appropriate sampling methods, such as wipe sampling or dust collection, to gather samples.
  3. Analysis: Sending collected samples to certified laboratories for analysis to determine lead concentration levels.
  4. Reporting: Providing comprehensive reports detailing lead concentration levels and recommendations for mitigation if necessary.

Benefits of Lead Sampling

  • Identification: Accurate identification of lead presence in various environments.
  • Risk Assessment: Assessment of potential health risks associated with lead exposure.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensuring compliance with lead-related regulations and guidelines.
  • Health Protection: Protecting occupants and workers from lead-related health hazards.
