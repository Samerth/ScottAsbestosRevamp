Lead Abatement

What is Lead Abatement?

Lead abatement is the process of reducing or eliminating lead hazards in the environment to prevent lead poisoning. This involves removing or covering lead-based paint, dust, and soil that may contain lead.

Our Lead Abatement Process

  1. Assessment: We conduct thorough inspections to identify lead hazards and assess the extent of contamination.
  2. Planning: Based on the assessment, we develop a comprehensive abatement plan tailored to the specific needs of the property.
  3. Abatement: Our trained professionals use specialized techniques and equipment to safely remove or contain lead hazards.
  4. Verification: After abatement, we conduct clearance testing to ensure that the area meets regulatory standards for lead exposure.
  5. Documentation: We provide detailed documentation of the abatement process, including test results and compliance certificates.

Benefits of Lead Abatement

  • Protects Health: Reduces the risk of lead poisoning and associated health problems.
  • Legal Compliance: Ensures compliance with regulations and standards for lead exposure.
  • Preserves Property Value: Improves property value by eliminating lead hazards and reducing liability.
  • Peace of Mind: Provides peace of mind knowing that the environment is safe from lead contamination.
